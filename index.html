Lelouch vi Britannia aka Zero

Lelouch vi Britannia is the main character of the anime series named Code Geass.

Lelouch is a high school student in alternate future version of an occupied Japan. In his world he is the disowned prince of the Britannian emperor, one of the rulers of the world. He is extremely intelligent and confident. Due to this he is vulnerable to episodes of grandeur and a God complex. He is also very popular, charismatic, and rebellious. So much so that he decides to moonlight as Zero the hero of the Japanese in their rebellion against Britannia.
